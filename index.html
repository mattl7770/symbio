Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 April 2026

Symbio (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Symbio mobile app and the website at symbioflat.com (together, the “Service”). We built Symbio to help flatmates live together better — fairly, collaboratively, and peacefully. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Account details: name, email address, password (or social login credentials), profile photo, and preferences.
  • Flat data: when you create or join a flat you share rules, chores, expenses, receipts, calendar events, shopping lists, chat messages, announcements, and dispute/evidence submissions (photos, descriptions, etc.).
  • Communications you send us (support messages).

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data: device type, operating system, IP address, app version, and interaction data (which features you use most).
  • Cookies and similar technologies on the website (for analytics and basic functionality).

We do not collect precise geolocation unless you explicitly enable it for reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service (manage your flat, calculate expense splits, rotate chores, send reminders and push notifications).
  • To power the AI Mediator — we process the evidence and rules you submit to generate neutral, fact-based suggestions. The AI does not store or learn from your data beyond the current dispute.
  • To personalise your experience and improve Symbio (analytics).
  • To communicate with you (important updates, support).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

Within your flat

All members of the same flat can see the shared content you post (rules, chores, expenses, receipts, chat, disputes). This is the core of Symbio and is by design.

Service providers

We use Supabase (our hosting and database provider) and other trusted processors who help us run the app. They are contractually required to protect your data.

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights, or in the event of a business transfer.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security & Storage

We use industry-standard encryption and Supabase's security controls. Data is stored in secure cloud servers (primarily United States with possible EU/AU options via Supabase). While we work hard to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → this permanently deletes your personal data (flat-shared data remains visible to remaining flatmates until they also delete or leave).

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live you may have rights to: access, correct, delete, or export your data, object to processing, or withdraw consent.

  • New Zealand residents: Privacy Act 2020
  • EU/UK residents: GDPR
  • California residents: CCPA/CPRA

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@symbioflat.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Symbio is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside New Zealand. We use appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses where required).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you via the app or email and update the “Effective Date”.
